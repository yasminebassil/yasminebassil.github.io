Getting There

The house sits at the corner of Moreland Ave SE and Lyndale Dr SE. If you’re driving or coming by rideshare, please turn onto Lyndale Dr SE to park or be dropped off.

Parking

Free street parking

Street Parking

Drive down Moreland Ave SE and, when you reach the intersection with Lyndale Dr SE, turn right onto Lyndale — it’s a smaller gravel road. There’s free street parking along the left side of Lyndale (the same side as the house). Please try to avoid parking on both sides of the road.

If Lyndale is full, the neighborhood just behind the house has plenty of free street parking — Gilbert St SE is a good bet.

Drop-off

For Uber, Lyft, or other rideshare, ask your driver to turn right onto Lyndale Dr SE for drop-off. The house is the very first one on the street (a pretty soft blue color).
